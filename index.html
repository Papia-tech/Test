Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SharePlate

How does SharePlate work?
Restaurants list surplus food and nearby NGOs get instant notifications for safe pickup.
Is SharePlate free to use?
Yes, SharePlate is completely free for both restaurants and NGOs.
How is food safety maintained?
Only freshly cooked surplus food is listed, with strict pickup time limits.
